System Engineering Architecture

The Challenge: Develop a low-friction vintage product marketplace that dynamically updates stock counts upon purchase transactions and securely issues order details to customers. The system must prevent inventory overselling during simultaneous checkouts.

The Solution: Built on a Django backbone with PostgreSQL, using database-level locking and transaction blocks (`transaction.atomic()`). This ensures order updates and product decrement steps occur together. If a payment fails midway, the locked stock returns immediately to general visibility.

Business Impact: Fully automated billing and email delivery, removing manual sales tracking tasks. This saved weekly administration hours while preventing double-purchases on unique vintage jackets.

Production Testing Protocol

  • Payment Success Webhooks: Assured billing captures update ledger tables and trigger confirmation emails.
  • Payment Cancellation Hooks: Tested database rollbacks when transactions are abandoned at checkout.
  • Stock Depletion Checks: Confirmed items are hidden from store listings when quantity hits 0.
  • Checkout Resilience: Checked page refresh safety to prevent duplicate order creations.
  • Device Adaptability: Tested form and layout rendering on mobile iOS, Android, and widescreen viewports.
